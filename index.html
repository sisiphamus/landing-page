How It Works

Four models, one answer.

For complex requests, Pepper runs a multi-step pipeline to deliver thorough, accurate results.

A

Delegator

Analyzes your request and defines exactly what output is needed.

B

Knowledge Auditor

Reviews all stored memories — skills, knowledge, preferences, sites — and selects what's relevant.

C

Teacher

If knowledge gaps are found, creates new memory files and triggers a re-audit.

D

Executor

Takes the full context and does the real work — writing, coding, analyzing, or creating.

+

Post-Task Learner

Reviews what happened and saves useful insights for future requests.
